Solar System simulation

It keeps slowing down and speeding up because I'm adjusting the simulation timestep dynamically, depending on how much accuracy is required. The solver is a Runge Kutta fourth order implementation (with custom adaptive timesteps)
The size of the planets depends on their Z position (how close they are to the camera), because the entire simulation is running in 3D but I didn't habe the time to make a proper 3D display yet. :D
